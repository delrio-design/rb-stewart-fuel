About Us


R. B. Stewart Petroleum Products was started in 1932 in Brazoria County, Texas as a fuel consignee. Through the years the company has grown from those humble roots and is now a leading transporter of refined fuel products, serving the eastern half of the state of Texas.

Senior Leadership of the company includes a member of the founding Stewart family. Through the years the company has focused on the basic principles of taking care of our employees and our customers. As the company has grown, today encompassing more than 40 power units and close to 100 employees, safety, customer service and treating people as we would like to be treated have remained consistent core values of the company.

Our business continues to grow and is now in the process of expanding outside of our home State of Texas. We are always looking for the right people to join the R. B. Stewart family and help us further our proud tradition of excellence in the fuel distribution space.
