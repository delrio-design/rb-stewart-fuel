If you are interested in working for R.B. Stewart, please complete the employment application link at the bottom of this page.

Requirements

  • Must be able to pass pre-employment physical and drug screening

  • Present a valid Class A Commercial Driver’s License (391.11) from State of Domicile with X endorsement (tanker and Haz-mat)

  • Must be at least 23 years of age.

  • Application for employment must be completely filled out with 10 complete years of work history. Any gaps in employment will have to be verified. Discovery of false information on the application will eliminate the canididate.
