Fueling the Future


R.B. Stewart Petroleum Products, Inc. is a Texas based company that has been serving the fuel and transportation needs of our customers for over 85 years. Today our focus is on the transportation and logistics side of the industry. We serve independent distributors of all sizes and haul renewable fuels for some of the nation’s largest companies. Safety, customer service, technology, and creating long term opportunities for our employees are the core fundamentals that our business is built on.

Established in 1932 by Guy Stewart in Angleton, TX, we’ve gone from a hand full of small trucks to forty plus trucks and trailers. With rapid expansion ahead and exponential growth forecasted, we are continually looking for new members to join our family. We strive to be the best in the business and our success proves that we are on the right track. Do you have what it takes to drive the big yellow truck?
